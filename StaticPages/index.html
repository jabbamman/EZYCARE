CHILDREN COME FIRST

Your kids deserves the best kids care possible, that’s why at EZY Care you kids home care service will be actively provided by a knowledgeable and experienced professional.

WHY TO CHOOSE EZY CARE?

Schedule

A Professional nanny works full-time, part-time, or hourly with one family exclusively.

Experience

Professional nannies will generally have at least 5 years of childcare experience.

Education

A professional nanny will be certified in first aid and CPR. They may hold a college degree.

Job Duties

Professional are responsible for all things child related,providing a safe environment.
